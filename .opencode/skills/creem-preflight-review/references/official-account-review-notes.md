# Creem Account Review Notes

Source checked: `https://docs.creem.io/merchant-of-record` on 2026-05-18. The page title was `Account Reviews`.

## Official checklist highlights

- Product is ready for production; use Test Mode while still building.
- No false information: fake reviews, fake testimonials, or inflated user/customer counts are rejection risks.
- Privacy Policy and Terms of Service must be present and accessible.
- Product must be clearly visible and understandable from website or landing page.
- Product name should avoid trademark conflicts and consumer confusion.
- Pricing must be visible.
- Product must comply with acceptable use and not be on the prohibited list.
- Reachable customer support email is required.
- AI image/video generation products must integrate Creem Moderation API.

## Common change requests

- Support email mismatch between Business Details and website.
- Website inaccessible, password-protected, down, or returning errors.
- Missing Privacy Policy or Terms of Service.
- False information such as fake reviews, testimonials, or inflated user counts.
- Product not ready for live review.

## Review process

- Reviews typically take 24-48 hours; peak periods may take up to 72 hours.
- If changes are requested, fix them and use Request Re-Review from the Payout Account page.
- Include Store ID when contacting support.

## AI wrapper requirements

- Product brand identity must be distinct from underlying AI model brands.
- Do not use model brand names in the product name.
- Model names may be mentioned as supported models or integrations if phrased clearly.
- If marketing mentions model names, include an independence/non-affiliation disclaimer.
- Feature claims must match actual delivered functionality and model version.
- Disclose wrapper nature for third-party AI model interfaces.
- One clearly defined product per Creem integration.
- AI image/video generation products require Creem Moderation API.
- AI generation products should have an Acceptable Use Policy and ToS clauses prohibiting NSFW/explicit/suggestive content.

## Prohibited/high-risk examples

- Pornographic/NSFW content, NSFW chatbots, face-swap/deepfake/face manipulation.
- IPTV, physical goods, spyware, illegal or age-restricted products, regulated products/services.
- Third-party content downloaders/rippers that violate platform terms.
- Marketplaces, dating sites, PLR/MRR, counterfeit goods, homework/essay mills, MLM.
- NFT/crypto assets, in-game items/currencies tied to third-party games.

## Community case notes from user-provided screenshots

- False testimonials can be treated as fake even if real users said them, when they are not directly linked to the reviewed product or cannot be verified.
- Inactive social links such as empty Discord or inactive Twitter/X can trigger rejection.
- Product information mismatch, such as claiming one AI model/version while using another, can trigger rejection.
- Recommended response pattern: fix comprehensively, remain in test mode until approved, reply within 48 hours, and list concrete changes.
